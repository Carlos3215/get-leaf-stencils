How to Use Leaf Stencils: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Surface

Decide where you want to apply the stencil. Make sure the surface is clean and smooth for the best results.

Step 2: Secure the Stencil

Use painter's tape to secure your leaf stencil in place.

Step 3: Apply Paint

Using a stencil brush, gently dab paint onto the stencil. Apply an even layer for the best effect.

Step 4: Remove the Stencil

Once you’ve applied the paint, carefully remove the stencil while the paint is still wet.

Step 5: Clean and Reuse

After use, clean your stencil with warm soapy water. Stencillo's leaf stencils are durable and reusable.
